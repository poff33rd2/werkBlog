Problem: Low website reach, engagement, and customer retainment.

• Wrote SEO based page content, menu item names, and blog posts to increase search results opportunities.

• Designed a streamlined Wix frontend, reducing clutter and improving user experience.

• Built one-click online ordering and query-parameter “tap to order” flows, increasing
online orders after migrating from Popmenu to Wix Studio.

• Developed dynamic menu display with Velo code, pulling items directly from the
database to highlight popular dishes and simplify updates.

• Optimized site performance by processing and compressing images and video content.

• Led daily stand-up meetings (DSUs) to improve cross-team communication.

• Created and launched multiple email campaigns, driving higher customer retention and
menu engagement.

• Conducted 10+ in-person user tests; applied feedback to enhance mobile usability and
design decisions.
